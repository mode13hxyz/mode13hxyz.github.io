Mode 13h
 

mode 13h is a VGA video mode with a resolution of 320x200 with 256 colors. It was introduced in 1987 with the IBM PS/2, and became the de facto standard video mode for MS-DOS games in the 1990s.

It is also a zine celebrating and exploring the glory days of MS-DOS gaming.
